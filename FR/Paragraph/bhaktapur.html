Welcome to Bhaktapur!

-The Living Museum of Nepal

Bhaktapur is a historic city in the Kathmandu Valley of Nepal. Known for its ancient temples, traditional art, and rich Newari culture, it is one of the most beautiful cultural heritage sites in Nepal.

Main Attractions

Bhaktapur Durbar Square

A UNESCO World Heritage Site filled with temples and palaces.

Nyatapola Temple

The tallest pagoda-style temple in Nepal.

Culture & Festivals

Bisket Jatra Festival

Bhaktapur celebrates vibrant festivals like Bisket Jatra and preserves traditional pottery, wood carving, and mask dances.

Gai Jatra Festival

A traditional festival where families celebrate the memory of their deceased loved ones.

Hotels and Restaurants

Hotel Bhadgaon

A luxury hotel in Bhaktapur offering traditional Newari hospitality and modern amenities located at the heart of the city.

Hotel Yechu

A comfortable hotel in Bhaktapur offering scenic views and excellent service.
