Welcome to Mustang!

-The Land of the Gods

Mustang is a historic region in northern Nepal. Known for its unique culture, stunning landscapes, and ancient monasteries, it is one of the most beautiful and culturally rich regions in Nepal.

Main Attractions

Muktinath Temple

A sacred Hindu temple in Mustang, known for its unique architecture and spiritual significance.

Dhumbalake

A scenic lake in Mustang, known for its natural beauty and cultural significance.

Culture & Festivals

Tiji Festival

A vibrant festival celebrated in Mustang, showcasing local traditions and culture.

Lhochar or Yartung

A traditional festival in Mustang, celebrated with local dances and rituals.

Hotels and Restaurants

Mustang Panorama Inn

A traditional hotel in Mustang offering authentic local hospitality and comfort.

Hotel De Purang

A luxury hotel in Mustang offering panoramic views and excellent amenities.
