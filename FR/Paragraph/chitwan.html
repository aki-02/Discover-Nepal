Welcome to Chitwan!

-The Garden of Nepal

Chitwan is a historic region in southern Nepal. Known for its lush forests, wildlife, and traditional culture, it is one of the most beautiful natural heritage sites in Nepal.

Main Attractions

Chitwan National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its rich biodiversity and wildlife.

Chitwan Tharu Village

A traditional Tharu village showcasing the unique culture and lifestyle of the Tharu people.

Culture & Festivals

Tharu Dance Festival

A vibrant festival showcasing the traditional dances and music of the Tharu community.

Elephant Festival

A traditional festival where elephant parades, games, and cultural performances highlight the elephants intelligence and strength.

Hotels and Restaurants

Green Park Chitwan

A luxury resort in Chitwan offering scenic views and excellent hospitality.

Jungle World Resort

A luxury resort in Chitwan offering jungle experiences and excellent hospitality.
