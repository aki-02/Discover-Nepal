Welcome to Kathmandu!

-The Capital City of Nepal

Kathmandu is the capital city of Nepal. Known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning architecture, It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Nepal.

Main Attractions

Basantapur Durbar Square

A UNESCO World Heritage Site filled with temples and palaces.

Swayambhunath Stupa

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most sacred Buddhist sites in Nepal.

Culture & Festivals

Indrajatra Festival

A traditional festival celebrated in Kathmandu with elaborate processions and cultural performances.

Holi Festival

A vibrant festival of colors celebrated in Kathmandu and across Nepal.

Hotels and Restaurants

Radisson Hotel

A luxury hotel in the heart of Kathmandu offering excellent amenities and services.

Kathmandu Boutique Hotel

A charming boutique hotel offering a unique and personalized experience in the heart of Kathmandu.
