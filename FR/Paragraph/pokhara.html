Welcome to Pokhara!

-The Lake City of Nepal

Pokhara is a historic city in the western part of Nepal. Known for its stunning natural beauty, including Phewa Lake and the Annapurna mountain range, it is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Nepal.

Main Attractions

Phewa Lake

The largest lake in Pokhara, offering scenic views and water activities.

Devi's Fall

A beautiful waterfall located near Phewa Lake in Pokhara.

Culture & Festivals

Dashain Festival

The most important festival in Nepal, celebrated with great enthusiasm and joy.

Lakhey Dance

A traditional dance performed during festivals in Pokhara, showcasing local culture and heritage.

Hotels and Restaurants

Pokhara Boutique Hotel

A luxury boutique hotel in Pokhara offering exceptional comfort and service.

Hotel Barahi

A traditional hotel in Pokhara offering authentic local hospitality and comfort.
