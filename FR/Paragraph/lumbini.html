About Lumbini

Lumbini is a historic site in Nepal where Lord Buddha was born. It is one of the most sacred Buddhist pilgrimage sites in the world, attracting millions of visitors and pilgrims annually.

Main Attractions

Mayadevi Temple

The temple where Lord Buddha was born, located in Lumbini.

World Peace Pagoda

A pagoda-style structure built to promote peace and harmony.

Culture & Festivals

Buddha Purnima Festival

A traditional festival celebrating the birth, enlightenment, and death of Lord Buddha.

Indian Nepal Cultural Festival

A vibrant festival showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Nepal and importance of it.
